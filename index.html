'Spotify mapped' - personal

This map shows how much me (green), and two other people (yellow and blue) listened to various artists from around the world.

Given that we had significantly different total listening times, the size is based on % of respective listening time. For artists two or more of us listened to, I have used the displace feature as you zoom in, to separate out the points so it's clearer who listened how much.

The lines between artists show collaborations between artists we listened to a lot, and I used a subtle heat map effect to make areas where we listened a lot glow

I got the artist location data by using UIPath to automate scraping data from Google and Wikipedia. In general I use birthplace or band origin. However for some artists I have manualy changed this to more accurately reflect their cultural background.
